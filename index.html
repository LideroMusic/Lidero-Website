LIDERO

Lidero is a 19 years old DJ and producer from Bulgaria. I'm an 19 years old DJ and producer from Bulgaria. He has started DJing at the age of 13 and his big passion is EDM music. His typical genres are Big Room House and Progressive House but he also does Pop, Edm and others music genres. His big dream is to be on the DJ MAG's TOP 100 DJ's list and perform on the biggest stages in the world!

Lidero has been involed in music production for over four years, lifting off his career by making remixes and bootlegs. He is self-taught producer and from a very early age has been listening to EDM music and watching the big DJs playing on the biggest stages and festivals in the world. Lideros'career has started in 2017 with his bootleg of "Let Me Love You" by Justin Bieber and DJ Snake which entered the Spinnin' Records Top 100 charts.

With over four years in the industry already, Lidero was able to win a couple of international remix competitions and produce for the biggest labels in the world. As well as major stream success on streaming platforms such as: Spotify, Apple Music, SoundCloud, etc, Lidero promises to present the best out of his music in 2022.
